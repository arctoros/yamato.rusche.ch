Tetra
When I started playing Tetris, I opted for tetr.io, the go-to platform for modern Tetris. However, after spending many hours playing there, I found the site to be heavy and sluggish, so I tried to recreate it myself to see if I could do any better.

Some parts are sadly unfinished and buggy due to a lack of time and fading interest. Also, I've found that it's quite hard to recreate the quick and snappy movement in tetr.io.

Nevertheless, I have attached the link and the files below for anyone who might want to try it out for themselves.
