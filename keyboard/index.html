Keyboard
My first custom keyboard was the GMMK Pro by Glorious, but I wasn’t fully content with it.

So when the opportunity came to join the Space65 R3 group buy in the spring of '22, I did:

Although the tactile switches have a relatively high pitch, it's muted by all the foam. The stabilised keys, though, are still quite deep and thocky.

All Parts:
Space65 R3 by GrayStudio
Redacted by GMK
Neapolitan Ice Cream by Tecsee
→ filmed and lubed (Tribosys 3203)
Gateron V2 Screw-In Stabilizers
→ lubed (Krytox 205g0)
